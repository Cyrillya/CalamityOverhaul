The Calamity Overhaul Mod aims to comprehensively optimize various aspects of the Calamity Mod. 

The current version has undergone redesigns for hundreds of Calamity Mod weapons, making them more refined, practical, and eye-catching. 

Additionally, adjustments have been made to hundreds of aspects of the Calamity Mod, including the addition of more craftable recipes and extensive numerical balancing to provide a smoother gaming experience. 

This mod introduces multiple new expanded weapons along with a brand-new crafting system to further enrich the gameplay progression. 

Future updates will continue to reset a significant number of Calamity Mod weapons and mechanics, bringing players a fresh Calamity experience.

The Mod is Open-Source In Github: https://github.com/hocha113/CalamityOverhaul
